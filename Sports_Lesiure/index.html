Welcome to Sport and Leisure

Founded in 2023, our aim has always been to offer great products at great prices for people who love sport, just as much as we do. We are entirely based online, which means, the money we save for not having a physical store, we reflect in our prices, so that our customers have the best deals and savings on our products. Here at Sport and Leisure, we offer a wide range of bikes and products for cyclists, from all the parts you’ll need if your bike needs fixing, to the accessories you might want to add, like a bell or a sticker. As well as all of that, we also offer products for different sports such Triathlons, motocross and snow sports, or maybe you just want some clothing for your gym session, we have that too. Along with providing all the gear and equipment you’ll ever need as a cyclist, we also make sure to get it to you promptly. If you order with us before 2pm, we will dispatch your order the very same day! Alternatively, you can collect your order, usually from as soon as an hour after you have purchased it by choosing the click and collect option at checkout. These are just some of the reasons to shop with us here at Sport and Leisure.
